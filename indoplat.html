Experience in PT INDOPLAT PERKASA PURNAMA

COMPANY PROFILE

Founded in 1985, our company has established a strong reputation as one of the leading manufacturers in the Plating on Plastic, Plastic Injection, Vacuum Metalizing, Hot Stamping, Painting on plastic, and Mould-Maintenance industries in Indonesia. We currently operate two factory sites, located in Jakarta and Karawang, which are fully equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and machinery. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering high-quality products and services to our customers, and we continuously strive to improve our operations and processes to ensure that we remain at the forefront of our industry.

Over the years, we have continuously grown and expanded our operations to meet the increasing demands of our customers. Our clients include Astra Honda Motor, Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Indonesia, Toyota Motor Manufacturing Indonesia, Mercedes Benz Indonesia, Suzuki Indomobil Motor, Yamaha Music Manufacturing Indonesia, Polytron, Sharp, and others. We are dedicated to meeting the evolving needs of our customers, and we look forward to continued growth and success in the years to come.
