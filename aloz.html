COMPANY PROFILE

PT Nittsu Shoji Indonesia is a company engaged in export and import logistics. This logistics-oriented company provides comprehensive logistics services including transportation, unloading, installation of imported and exported equipment, packaging, shipping, and customs clearance synchronized through a large logistics system.

PT Nittsu Shoji Indonesia manages logistics according to standards, covering products, weight, and appropriate distribution methods. The company is affiliated with PT Nittsu Lemo Indonesia, PT Nippon Express Indonesia, and PT Nex Logistik Indonesia. Particularly in trading systems, exports, and imports utilizing Third Party Logistics (3PL) services.
