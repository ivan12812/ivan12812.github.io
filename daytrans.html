Experience in PT DAY TRANS

COMPANY PROFILE

Day Trans is a company providing land transportation services, light goods delivery, and travel services, which was acquired by PT. Panorama Transportasi, Tbk. in October 2009. Under the management of White Horse Group, Day Trans has grown and developed alongside its customers through reliable, high-quality, and professional services



POSITION

Administrator (Reservation Staff)



LENGTH OF SERVICE

January 2025 to April 2025



JOBDESC

1. Receive and Process Reservation

  • Assist customers who wish to book land transportation tickets and travel services.
  • Accurately enter reservation data into the system.
  • Confirm reservations and provide travel details to customers.

2. Provide Information to Customers

  • Answer inquiries regarding travel schedules, fares, routes, and company policies.
  • Provide information on schedule changes, cancellations, or refund policies.

3. Manage Payments and Administration

  • Process ticket payments via cash, transfer, or other methods as per procedure.
  • Issue tickets or reservation receipts to customers.
  • Record transactions and prepare daily/weekly reports.

4. Handle Customer Complaints and Requests

  • Assist customers experiencing issues with their booking or travel.
  • Resolve problems quickly and provide the best solutions based on company policies.

5. Coordinate with the Operational Team

  • Work closely with the operational team regarding fleet availability and departure schedules.
  • Ensure vehicle capacity aligns with the number of reservations received.

6. Maintain Service Quality

  • Provide friendly, professional, and informative service to customers.
  • Ensure customer satisfaction by maintaining good communication.

7. Maintain Counter Cleanliness and Organization

  • Keep the work area clean and comfortable for customers.
  • Arrange documents, forms, or work equipment properly after use.
