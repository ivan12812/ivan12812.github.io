Experience in PT DAY TRANS

COMPANY PROFILE

Day Trans is a company providing land transportation services, light goods delivery, and travel services, which was acquired by PT. Panorama Transportasi, Tbk. in October 2009. Under the management of White Horse Group, Day Trans has grown and developed alongside its customers through reliable, high-quality, and professional services



POSITION

Counter Staff



LENGTH OF SERVICE

January 2025 to September 2025



JOBDESC

1. Serving Customers at the Counter

  • Greet customers who come to the counter.
  • Assist customers in purchasing shuttle tickets.
  • Explain departure schedules, prices, routes, seat options, and terms & conditions.
  • Accept ticket payments

2. Ticket Reservation / Sales Process & Package Handling

  • Receive and process ticket reservations, either in-person, via phone, or online if handling reservations.
  • Input customer data into the reservation system.
  • Print tickets or provide e-tickets.
  • Receive packages from customers and record shipping details: address, weight/volume, route, and rates.
  • Explain estimated delivery time and any additional fees (if applicable).

3. Customer Service

  • Respond to customer inquiries regarding services, routes, departures, or schedule changes.
  • Handle complaints: delays, cancellations, damaged items (if any), or booking errors.
  • Provide information on promotions or additional services if available.

4. Administration & Reporting

  • Manage daily transaction data at the counter.
  • Prepare ticket/package sales reports.
  • Organize ticket stock, shipping forms, and counter supplies.
  • Keep items, tickets, and receipts neatly arranged.

5. Counter Cleanliness & Personal Appearance

  • Maintain cleanliness of the counter area.
  • Maintain a neat and professional personal appearance (as this role is front-line facing customers).
